A Giggle a Day Keeps the Stress Away

Laughter uplifts the spirit and alleviate the burdens of daily life. It is a universal language that transcends barriers. Through laughter exercises, playful activities, and humor-infused interactions, laughter therapy triggers the release of endorphins—nature's natural stress-busters—resulting in an immediate boost in mood and a reduction in stress levels. By engaging in intentional laughter, individuals can temporarily escape the grip of stress, providing respite for both the mind and body.
